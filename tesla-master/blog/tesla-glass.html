TESLA ARMOR GLASS

Tesla Cybertruck

Cybertruck’s windows are made of armor glass that’s way more durable than the usual stuff used for pickup trucks, but when von Holzhausen threw a metal ball at the driver-side window, the glass cracked into a shatter pattern. During pre-show tests, we threw wrenches, we threw everything, we even literally threw the kitchen sink at the glass, and it didn’t break.
